合适的场景

The Future of the Industry: A Market with Immense Potentia

With the global expansion of 5G networks, the increasing penetration of smartphones, and the growing dependence on mobile devices, the mobile power bank industry is poised for explosive growth. Industry experts predict that by 2030, the global market for mobile power bank rental services will reach $10 billion. In response to this immense potential, Jiuguo Technology has already established branch offices in Shanghai, Shenzhen, Guangzhou, Hong Kong, Thailand, Germany and Malaysia. Through our global presence, we provide comprehensive solutions for mobile power bank rental services to our clients worldwide.

Company Overview

Shenzhen Jiuguo Technology Co., Ltd. is a high-tech enterprise specializing in the R&D, production, sales, and operation of shared power banks. Backed by technologies such as big data, artificial intelligence, and smart hardware, as well as extensive experience in operating over one million shared devices, we provide comprehensive OEM/ODM software and hardware solutions for global clients. Our R&D headquarters is located in Shenzhen, while our operations headquarters is based in Shanghai. Additionally, we have branch offices in Guangzhou, Hong Kong, Thailand, and Malaysia. The core members of our team come from renowned industry leaders such as Meituan, ZhuMang, and Laidian, with over six years of professional experience in the field, bringing extensive expertise in both operations and R&D. Our team spearheaded the development and successful deployment of the world’s first shared power bank. Having witnessed the industry’s evolution from its infancy (0) to growth (1) and maturity (10), we bring ten years of shared power bank R&D experience and eight years of operational expertise to support our clients’ growth in multiple dimensions. We adhere to the philosophy of being product-oriented and customer-focused, continuously driving technological development and product innovation to provide premium, one-stop solutions for users worldwide.
